HOW TO DESIGN ONE ALGORITHM
─────────────────────────────

  1. WHAT IS THE PROBLEM?
     Given   [what you have + constraints]
     Find    [what you want + success condition]

  2. WHAT ARE THE BOUNDARY CASES?
     What input breaks the postcondition?
     What must be true for the answer to exist?

  3. WHAT MODEL?
     sequential | parallel | reactive

  4. DOES IT TERMINATE?
     yes — what is the decreasing measure?
     no  — what guarantees responsiveness?

  5. WRITE THE STEPS
     For each step ask:
       - what operation is this?  (from Layer 2)
       - what is the invariant after this step?
       - where does control go next?

  6. VERIFY
     Termination  — proven by measure or fixed step count
     Correctness  — postcondition holds at output
     Invariant    — true at entry of every step
